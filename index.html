Hi, my name is Alexa! First off, I want to say thank you for visting my site & I cannot wait to create something beautiful together. I am a freelance web developer and designer. I recieved my bachelor's degree in Studio Arts at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee. During and after my studies I worked for a large company that taught me a lot, but ultimately did not allow me to live the lifestyle I wanted. So I decided to learn a new skill that accompanied my love for art and design whilst providing that freedom I craved.

I am proudly Latina which means I can also curate sites in Spanish. If you have a Mexican restaurant, I will gladly exchange a site for a large supply of tacos! I have a wonderful family back home composed of my lovely parents and three crazy brothers. In my free time here in Bali I enjoy heading to the beach for a surf, drawing, and eating my way through Bali's amazing food scene.
